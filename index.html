MiniChat Seller is a chatbot that automatically responds to messages on our Facebook Page via Messenger. It helps provide product information, receive customer orders, and summarize order details through automated conversations.

  • Platform: Facebook Messenger only
  • No login or app installation required

üéØ Purpose of the App:

  • To automatically answer customer inquiries
  • To collect order details (product, quantity, name, address, payment method)
  • To summarize and confirm orders before fulfillment

üîí Private Use Notice:

This chatbot processes only the information shared voluntarily by users during chat interactions for order fulfillment. No sensitive data is stored or shared outside the Facebook environment.
